Welcome to the EreshBot Calculation Wiki!

What's Changed In The New Command?

Command Chain Calculations

One of the largest changes is that you can now calculate multiple cards at once. With the old command, you had to calculate each card individually. Not only was this extremely tedious, but calculating each card individually wouldn't take things like FGO's famous overkill bug into account. Now, you can calculate an entire chain at once!

Multiple Enemies, Multiple Waves

You can also now calculate multiple enemies at once! Want to see what a Noble Phantasm will do against an entire wave of enemies? No problem! Curious if your three turn looping comp will refund enough over all three waves? You can calculate that! Need to know if your fancy team for a challenge quest can get the job done? You know what to do.

And Much More!

You can now calculate critical star generation in addition to NP generation and damage. Curious just how many stars your Jack the Ripper will be able to generate next turn? EreshBot can calculate that.

The verbose page is much improved, and allows for a finer understanding of exactly where each buff is coming from and the generation stats of each hit on the card.

There are many, many more arguments at your disposal than there were before! Bring your calcs to the next level!
